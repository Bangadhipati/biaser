Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science Engineering

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Computer Science Engineering at BIASER offers a comprehensive curriculum blending theory with practical applications. Students delve into core computer science concepts such as algorithms, data structures, and software engineering, preparing them for careers at the forefront of technology innovation. With state-of-the-art facilities and expert faculty guidance, graduates emerge equipped to tackle complex challenges in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Aerospace Engineering

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Aerospace Engineering at BIASER offers a comprehensive curriculum, blending theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare students for careers in the aerospace industry. Through specialized coursework and hands-on training, students gain expertise in aircraft design, propulsion systems, aerodynamics, and space technology. With state-of-the-art facilities and expert faculty guidance, this program equips graduates with the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in the dynamic field of aerospace engineering.

Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Computer Science Engineering

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) program in Computer Science Engineering at BIASER offers an advanced curriculum tailored to meet the evolving demands of the tech industry. Students delve into cutting-edge topics such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science, gaining hands-on experience through practical projects and research initiatives. Led by renowned faculty and equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, this program prepares graduates to excel as leaders and innovators in the dynamic field of computer science engineering.
